About Me

I am a scholar dedicated to the study of Ancient Chinese Literature, with a focus on the Yuan, Ming, and Qing dynasties. My work involves digital humanities approaches, including the creation of academic maps and social relationship networks for historical figures. I am proficient in the erhu and deeply engaged in traditional Chinese culture.
